Questions? Answered.

Wait, $19/month for unlimited users? Really?

Really. No per-seat pricing, no user limits on the Pro plan. Add your whole team. Postman charges $14 per seat per month — a team of 5 costs $70/month. DevBook Pro is $19/month for everyone.

What happens after the 14-day trial?

If you upgrade to Pro, you keep everything. If you don't, you drop to the Free plan — you keep all your templates and keys, but sharing turns off and you're limited to 25 templates and 10 keys.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel with one click, no questions asked. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your billing period, then drop to Free.

Do I need a credit card to start the trial?

No. Sign up, start using Pro features immediately. We'll ask for payment details when the trial ends.

How do you make money at $19/month?

DevBook is intentionally simple — it doesn't require massive infrastructure or a large team. Low overhead means we can charge less and still build a sustainable business. No VC, no growth-at-all-costs pressure.

Is my data safe?

API keys are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM. All connections use TLS. We never log request payloads or API responses. Your data stays in your account and is never shared or sold.

What if I'm the only developer on my team?

Use the Free plan — it's genuinely free forever. You get 25 templates, 10 API keys, and all core features. Upgrade to Pro only when you need team sharing or unlimited storage.
